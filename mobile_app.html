eBrickkiln Mobile App

Reduce your data entry workload efficiently with our mobile app.

Unilead introduces eBrickkiln, the first comprehensive product designed specifically for the brick kiln industry, addressing every conceivable requirement of brick kiln owners. This innovative solution brings true automation to the industry, allowing users to enter data both globally and individually, thereby reducing office workload and saving money.
Unilead offers a pioneering mobile app eBrickkiln designed for the convenience of brick kiln owners. This app allows for seamless data entry from mobile devices, making it easier for owners to manage their operations on the go.



Key features of the eBrickkiln mobile app include:

  • Raw Material Management: Track and manage material transactions such as mitti (clay) and sand.
  • Pather Work: Punch / enter the raw brick molding work.
  • Kharaba/Pamayash-User can enter the Kharaba entry from Mobile APP.
  • Kachi Bharyee: Daily Bharayee Entry Raw Brick From Pather or Stock Jalayee
  • Jalayee- Daily Consumption Entry of Burinig Item during jalayee.
  • Nikashi: Track the removal of finished bricks from the kiln.
  • Challan Management: Generate and manage delivery challans and transportation documents.
  • Receipt and Payment Management: Enter and track financial transactions directly from the mobile app.
  • Mark Attendance: Salaried Class Employees: Effortlessly track attendance for salaried employees to streamline auto salary management. Ensure accurate payroll processing with minimal manual intervention.
  • Order Status Real-Time Tracking: Owners can easily monitor the status of orders, whether they are delivered, on the way, or pending. Stay updated on order progress and ensure timely deliveries.
  • Reminder System:Timely Alerts: The reminder feature helps you complete tasks on time, avoiding delays and penalties. Stay organized and ensure critical activities are never overlooked.

By integrating these features, eBrickkiln ensures brick kiln owners can efficiently manage their operations, reduce administrative burdens, and improve overall productivity. This makes eBrickkiln an essential tool for modernizing brick kiln businesses and maintaining a competitive edge in the industry
